Fonts & UI cards

Mockup

This assignment was all about fonts and UI cards. I first researched a display font and a paragraph font I thought went well together. After that I applied the fonts. The bigger fonts like h1 and h2 have the display font applied however as I implemented the font hierarchy I quickly noticed that h3 and h4 would be unreadable as a display font and therefore changed those to my paragraph font. Call to action buttons and links are all in a paragraph font because they should be easy and fast to read. I used the unit rem for fonts, line height, margins etc. as this is also a big part of setting type.

Details

When it came to my card design I have chosen a muted pink and given them a shadow to make the cards stand out from the white background. I have all my cards in a card container that is in a grid layout. The cards themselves are designed with flexbox. This is because grid is generally better for layouts and flexbox is generally better for components. I have applied font hierarchy in my cards and "grouped" the different information to make it more readable and comprehensible. I have also applied colors on my buttons so they clearly indicate their intention.
